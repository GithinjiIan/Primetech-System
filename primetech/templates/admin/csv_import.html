Bulk Learner CSV Import

Upload a CSV file to create student accounts and enroll them into courses in bulk.

Required CSV columns:
  • full_name — Student's full name
  • email — Email address (used for login)
  • course_id — Course ID (find it in the Courses list)
Optional columns:
  • phone_number, nationality, gender (male/female)

New accounts will receive a welcome email with temporary login credentials. Existing accounts will just be enrolled (no duplicate email sent).

Example row:

full_name,email,course_id,phone_number,nationality,gender
Jane Doe,jane@example.com,3,+254700000000,Kenyan,female
John Smith,john@example.com,3,+254712345678,Ugandan,male
